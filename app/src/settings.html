⚙️ Settings

Nothing here changes what's applied to any target — this is the app's own local configuration.

Danger Map reporting

Off by default. Nothing about a close call ever leaves this machine without this box checked, and even then only pattern-level fields go out — never prompts, paths, or file contents. Turning this on does not submit anything by itself: it only lets the Mouse Tray offer a report to queue for your review, one at a time, still requiring its own separate approval before it's ever sent.

Remote alarm webhook

Optional. When set, a tripwire-class event (the tray going to its "mouse" state) posts to this URL. Leave blank to disable.

Skin

Pick among the skins you've unlocked. Locked ones show what's still out there to find.
